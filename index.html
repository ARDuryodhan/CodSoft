About Us

Welcome to Pet Planet - Where Pets Reign Supreme!

Established in 2020 by passionate pet enthusiast Aditya Duryodhan, Pet Planet is not just a pet shop; it's a haven for pet lovers and their furry companions. Aditya's vision was to create a space that goes beyond traditional pet stores, offering a holistic and delightful experience for pets and their owners alike.

Our Mission:

At Pet Planet, our mission is to enhance the well-being of your pets by providing top-quality products and services. We understand the unique bond you share with your furry friends, and we're committed to nurturing that connection through a range of offerings.

Servies

Pet Boarding or Kennel Attendants

Pet boarding involves providing a temporary home for pets when their owners are away. Kennel attendants are responsible for the daily care of boarded pets, ensuring they are fed, exercised, and receive any necessary medications. They also monitor the pets' well-being and provide companionship.

Pet Training

Pet training services involve teaching pets essential obedience commands, behavior modification, and sometimes specialized skills. Trainers work with pets and their owners to address behavioral issues, enhance communication, and create a positive relationship between the pet and owner.

Veterinary Acupuncturist

A veterinary acupuncturist specializes in using acupuncture, an ancient Chinese medicine practice, to treat various health issues in animals. This can include pain management, arthritis, and other conditions. Acupuncture involves inserting thin needles into specific points on the pet's body to stimulate energy flow and promote healing.

Dog or Cat Breeder / Assistant

Breeders are responsible for carefully selecting and pairing animals to produce specific breeds. They ensure the health, temperament, and genetic traits of the animals meet breeding standards. Breeders may also assist in the birthing process and early care of the newborn animals.

Pet Grooming

Pet grooming services involve maintaining the cleanliness and overall health of pets. This includes bathing, brushing, trimming nails, and cleaning ears. Groomers may also identify skin issues or other health concerns during the grooming process.

Pet Sitting

Pet sitting provides an alternative to boarding by offering in-home care for pets. Pet sitters visit the pet's home to feed them, provide companionship, and attend to their basic needs. This service is especially beneficial for pets that may feel stressed in a new environment or for owners who prefer their pets to stay at home.

These services collectively contribute to the well-being and care of pets, addressing their physical, emotional, and medical needs.
